    Category Guidelines

    Naming Convention
    Use clear, descriptive names that represent broad device types. Examples: "Computers", "Network Equipment", "Storage Devices"
    Category Codes
    Keep codes short (2-10 characters) and use uppercase letters. Examples: "COMP", "NET", "STOR", "PRINT"
    Organization Tips
    Categories should represent main device types. Use subcategories for more specific classifications like "Laptops" under "Computers"
